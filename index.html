Cupid's Arrow

This is a group project. Our group aimed to develop a game for social change. Our goal is to create an engaging experience that makes players think critically about the potential dangers of online dating. “Cupid’s Arrow” is a text-based game with interactive elements and multiple endings. The story structure is “Branching Structure”.

The Darkest Venture

This is a group project. Our group aimed to develop a game to provide audiences an immersive experience. “The Darkest Venture” is a horror adventure game with interactive narratives and puzzles. The story structure is “Branching Structure” and we focused on player agency and the interactive elements for better experience.
